# Instruction Decoding

Registers:
B = 000
C = 001
D = 010
E = 011
H = 100
L = 101
M = 110
A = 111

00 DDD 110: MVI
01 DDD SSS: MOV

01 110 110: HLT

10 000 DDD: ADD
10 001 DDD: ADC
10 010 DDD: SUB
10 011 DDD: SBB
10 100 DDD: ANA
10 101 DDD: XRA
10 110 DDD: ORA
10 111 DDD: CMP

11 000 110: ADI
11 001 110: ADC
11 010 110: SUB
11 011 110: SBB
11 100 110: ANA
11 101 110: XRA
11 110 110: ORA
11 111 110: CMP
